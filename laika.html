On November 3, 1957 Laika was launched into outer space with Sputnik 2 spacecraft

Little was known about the impact of spaceflight on living creatures.

Therefore Soviet Union made an experiment aimed to prove that a living passenger could survive being launched into orbit

Side Fact
Before the launch, the sensors showed Laika's heart rate was 103 beats/min. During the early acceleration, her heart rate increased to 240 beats/min

Laika was the first living animal that lauched into the orbit
